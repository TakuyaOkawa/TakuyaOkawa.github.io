Research Interests

Indirect dark matter, axion, and sterile neutrino searches

My past work targeted $\mu$eV QCD axion dark matter (2311.13653), MeV axion-like particles (2412.21163, slides), sterile neutrino dark matter, and relic neutrinos (2408.01484). I also work on dark matter captured by celestial objects (slides).

Light scalar dynamics in the early universe

I studied the production of a baryon number by the Affleck-Dine mechanism within the curvaton scenario (2410.13712). I am also currently working on a more minimal curvaton mechanism by incorporating the thermal effects (slides).

$\boldsymbol{\nu}$

Dirac-Majorana nature of neutrinos

I investigate how to distinguish Dirac from Majorana neutrinos by using energy and angular distributions of their decay products in neutrino beam experiments (slides).
